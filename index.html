Privacy

Effective May 17, 2026

Ghosting Studios operates the websites and applications listed above. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what we do with it.

What we collect

  • Account data — for products that require an account: email address, and a hashed password if applicable.
  • Email forwards — for inboxzombie and zombiesub: the contents of emails you forward to our capture addresses, processed to extract subscription details.
  • Payment data — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your full card number.
  • Usage data — standard server logs (IP, user agent, timestamps) retained up to 30 days for security and abuse prevention.

What we don't do

  • We do not link bank accounts.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for advertising.
  • We do not use third-party tracking pixels or behavioural advertising cookies.

Third parties we use

  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI extraction from forwarded receipts; contents sent for a single extraction call only
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Cloudflare — hosting and edge infrastructure

Your rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing hello@ghostingstudios.com. We respond within 14 days.
